Most orders are completed within 1–5 minutes after successful payment.

We accept eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay, Bank Transfer and other available payment methods.

If your order is delayed, please contact us with your payment proof. We will check and resolve it as soon as possible.

Orders cannot be cancelled once they have been processed.
